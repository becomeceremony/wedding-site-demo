Our Story

We met in a way neither of us expected.
It started small, almost unnoticeable at first.
But something about it felt different immediately.
We talked longer than we ever meant to.
Then those conversations became daily.
Then daily became natural.
And natural became necessary.
We grew through school, stress, and life changes.
We learned patience, timing, and understanding.
We saw each other at our best and worst.
And never stopped choosing each other.
Somewhere along the way, it stopped being “dating.”
It became building a life.
A shared rhythm.
A shared direction.
A shared future.
Now we are ready for the next chapter.
And we are grateful to share it with you.
